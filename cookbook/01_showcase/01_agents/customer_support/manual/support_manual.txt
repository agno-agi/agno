# Customer Support Knowledge Base

This document contains common support topics, FAQs, and resolution guides.

---

## BILLING

### Duplicate Charges
**Issue:** Customer reports being charged twice for the same subscription or order.
**Resolution:**
1. Verify the duplicate charge in the billing system
2. Check if it's an authorization hold vs actual charge (holds typically clear in 3-5 business days)
3. If confirmed duplicate, process refund immediately
4. Apologize for the inconvenience and confirm refund timeline (3-5 business days for credit cards)
5. Offer a small credit or discount for the trouble (10% off next purchase)

### Refund Policy
**Standard Policy:**
- Full refund within 30 days of purchase for unused products
- Prorated refund for subscription cancellations
- No refund for digital products after download/access
- Shipping costs are non-refundable unless item arrived damaged

**Processing Times:**
- Credit card: 3-5 business days
- PayPal: 1-3 business days
- Bank transfer: 5-7 business days

### Subscription Changes
**Upgrades:**
- Take effect immediately
- Prorated billing for the remainder of the cycle
- New features available instantly

**Downgrades:**
- Take effect at next billing cycle
- No partial refunds for current cycle
- Access to premium features continues until cycle ends

### Payment Methods
**Accepted:**
- Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- PayPal
- Apple Pay, Google Pay
- Bank transfer (enterprise only)

**Failed Payment Handling:**
1. Automatic retry after 24 hours
2. Email notification sent
3. 3 retries over 7 days
4. Account suspended after all retries fail
5. 14-day grace period to update payment method

## TECHNICAL ISSUES

### Login Problems
**Cannot Log In:**
1. Verify email address is correct (check for typos)
2. Check Caps Lock is off
3. Try "Forgot Password" to reset
4. Clear browser cache and cookies
5. Try incognito/private browsing mode
6. Check if account is locked (5 failed attempts = 15 min lockout)

**Password Reset Email Not Received:**
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Add support@company.com to contacts
3. Verify email address on file is correct
4. Wait 5 minutes and try again
5. If still not received, contact support to verify email

**Two-Factor Authentication Issues:**
1. Use backup codes (provided during 2FA setup)
2. If no backup codes, identity verification required
3. Verification options: ID upload, security questions, phone verification

### Service Outages
**How to Check:**
- Status page: status.company.com
- Twitter: @CompanyStatus
- In-app status banner

**During Outage:**
- Acknowledge the issue and apologize
- Provide estimated resolution time if available
- Do not promise specific ETAs unless confirmed by engineering
- Offer credit for extended outages (>4 hours)

### Error Codes
**Error 500 - Internal Server Error:**
- Server-side issue, customer should wait and retry
- If persistent, escalate to engineering

**Error 503 - Service Unavailable:**
- Service overloaded or under maintenance
- Check status page, usually temporary

**Error 401 - Unauthorized:**
- Session expired, customer needs to log in again
- Check if password was recently changed

**Error 403 - Forbidden:**
- Account may be suspended or restricted
- Check account status and permissions

**Error 404 - Not Found:**
- Page or resource no longer exists
- Check URL for typos, or feature may have been removed

## ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT

### Account Deletion
**Process:**
1. Customer must request via account settings or email
2. 30-day grace period before permanent deletion
3. All data will be permanently removed
4. Cannot be undone after grace period
5. Subscription must be cancelled first

**Data Export:**
- Available in account settings
- Includes all personal data and activity history
- Delivered as JSON/CSV within 48 hours

### Profile Updates
**What Can Be Changed:**
- Email address (verification required)
- Password
- Display name
- Profile picture
- Notification preferences
- Timezone and language

**What Cannot Be Changed:**
- Username (after 30 days from creation)
- Account creation date
- Primary email without verification

### Security
**Password Requirements:**
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character
- Cannot be same as previous 5 passwords

**Suspicious Activity:**
- Unusual login locations trigger email alert
- Multiple failed logins trigger temporary lock
- Password change triggers notification to all devices

## SHIPPING AND DELIVERY

### Shipping Options
**Standard Shipping:**
- 5-7 business days
- Free on orders over $50
- $4.99 for orders under $50

**Express Shipping:**
- 2-3 business days
- $12.99 flat rate

**Overnight Shipping:**
- Next business day (orders before 2 PM)
- $24.99 flat rate
- Not available for P.O. boxes

### Tracking
**How to Track:**
- Email with tracking link sent when shipped
- Track in account under "Order History"
- Tracking updates every 24-48 hours

**Common Tracking Statuses:**
- "Label Created": Shipment ready, not yet with carrier
- "In Transit": On the way
- "Out for Delivery": Will arrive today
- "Delivered": Confirmed delivery

### Missing or Delayed Packages
**If Package Shows Delivered But Not Received:**
1. Check with neighbors
2. Check mailroom/front desk (apartments)
3. Look for delivery notice
4. Wait 24 hours (carrier sometimes marks early)
5. File claim after 48 hours

**If Package is Delayed:**
- Weather delays: Allow extra 1-3 days
- Carrier issues: Contact carrier directly
- 7+ days late: Reship or refund offered

### Address Changes
**Before Shipping:**
- Can change in account or contact support
- Free of charge

**After Shipping:**
- Contact carrier directly for intercept
- Additional fees may apply
- Not guaranteed to succeed

## RETURNS AND EXCHANGES

### Return Process
1. Initiate return in account or contact support
2. Receive prepaid shipping label via email
3. Pack item in original packaging
4. Drop off at carrier location
5. Refund processed within 5-7 days of receipt

### Conditions
**Eligible for Return:**
- Unused and in original packaging
- Within 30 days of delivery
- Proof of purchase required

**Not Eligible:**
- Worn, used, or damaged items
- Items without tags
- Final sale items
- Perishable goods
- Personalized items

### Exchanges
- Same process as returns
- Place new order separately
- Original return refunded when received
- Exchanges for different size/color only

### Damaged or Defective Items
- Report within 48 hours of delivery
- Photos required for documentation
- Replacement shipped immediately
- No need to return damaged item under $50 value

## ESCALATION PROCEDURES

### When to Escalate
**Immediate Escalation Required:**
- Customer requests supervisor/manager
- Legal threats or mentions of lawyer
- Media/social media threats
- Security or safety concerns
- Data breach or privacy issues
- Abusive behavior toward staff

**Consider Escalation:**
- Issue unresolved after 3+ contacts
- Policy exception may be warranted
- Multi-department involvement needed
- Customer is high-value (enterprise, long-term)

### Escalation Contacts
- **Tier 2 Support:** Complex technical issues
- **Billing Team:** Payment disputes, large refunds
- **Legal Team:** Legal threats, compliance issues
- **Security Team:** Account compromises, data concerns
- **Customer Success:** Enterprise accounts, retention

## TONE AND COMMUNICATION

### Response Templates

**Opening - Neutral:**
"Thank you for contacting us. I understand you're experiencing [issue]. I'm here to help."

**Opening - Frustrated Customer:**
"I'm truly sorry to hear about [issue]. I completely understand your frustration, and I want to make this right."

**Opening - Angry Customer:**
"I sincerely apologize for this experience. Your frustration is completely valid, and resolving this is my top priority."

**Closing:**
"Is there anything else I can help you with today? We appreciate your patience and your business."

### Words to Avoid
- "Unfortunately" (use "I understand" instead)
- "Policy" (explain the reason instead)
- "Can't" (use "what I can do is..." instead)
- "You should have" (never blame customer)
- "Calm down" (acknowledge instead)

### Empathy Statements
- "I understand how frustrating this must be."
- "I would feel the same way in your situation."
- "Thank you for your patience while we work on this."
- "I appreciate you bringing this to our attention."
- "Let me make sure we get this resolved for you."
