# Tax Policy Guide

## Overview

This document provides an overview of tax policies for the fiscal year 2024.

## Income Tax

Income tax rates apply to individual earnings above the standard deduction threshold.
The standard deduction for 2024 is $14,600 for single filers.

### Tax Brackets

The following tax brackets apply for 2024:
- 10% for income up to $11,600
- 12% for income from $11,601 to $47,150
- 22% for income from $47,151 to $100,525

### Deductions and Credits

Taxpayers may claim itemized deductions or the standard deduction,
whichever results in a lower tax liability.

## Corporate Tax

The corporate tax rate is a flat 21% on taxable income.
Corporations may carry forward net operating losses to offset future income.

### Filing Requirements

All corporations with gross receipts exceeding $250,000 must file annually.
Estimated tax payments are required quarterly.

## Sales Tax

Sales tax rates vary by jurisdiction and apply to retail transactions.
Exemptions exist for essential goods including groceries and medicine.
